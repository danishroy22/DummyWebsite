About Us

At Oracle Club, we are dedicated to bridging the gap between industry and academia, providing a platform for growth, learning, and enjoyment. As an IT club at the University of Mauritius, we pride ourselves on offering a diverse range of events and activities tailored to both IT enthusiasts and those interested in non-IT fields.

Bridging the Gap

Our mission is to connect students with industry professionals, fostering relationships that enhance career opportunities and industry knowledge.

Diverse Events

We host a variety of events, from technical workshops and coding competitions to social gatherings and non-IT related activities, ensuring there's something for everyone.

Student Growth

We are committed to the personal and professional growth of our members, providing resources, mentorship, and opportunities to develop new skills.

Enjoyment and Engagement

Beyond academics, we believe in creating an enjoyable and engaging university experience through our dynamic and inclusive events.

Join us at Oracle Club to be a part of a community that supports your academic journey and enriches your university life!
